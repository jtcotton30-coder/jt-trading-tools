I'm now on a focused path to transition into a data analyst role — combining my operational domain expertise (safety, workforce, compliance, logistics) with skills in SQL, Python, and data visualization. I bring something most data analysts don't have: I know how the floor works, what actually drives decisions, and how to build tools that real people use.
