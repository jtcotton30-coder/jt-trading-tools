Entry Signals (When to BUY)

📈
Price above VWAP + pulling back to touch VWAP = buy the bounce HIGH PROB
✂️
9 EMA crosses above 20 EMA on 5-min chart = momentum shift bullish BUY
💥
Breakout above resistance with volume > 2x average = strong entry BUY
🏁
Bull flag breakout — tight consolidation after sharp move up, breaks higher with volume BUY
🔄
Bounce off support with hammer candle + volume = reversal entry BUY
📊
RSI divergence — price making new low but RSI making higher low = hidden strength BUY
⏰
Opening Range Breakout — mark first 15min high/low. Break above high with volume = go long BUY
🔴

Exit Signals (When to SELL)

🎯
Hit profit target — pre-defined R:R reached (2:1 minimum). Take profit. Don't get greedy. SELL
🛑
Stop-loss hit — NO QUESTIONS. Exit immediately. Never move stop further away. SELL
📉
Price breaks below VWAP with volume after you're long = trend changing SELL
✂️
9 EMA crosses below 20 EMA = momentum dying. Take remaining profit or cut loss. SELL
🕯️
Bearish engulfing candle at resistance on high volume = sellers taking over SELL
📊
RSI above 70 + price at resistance + volume fading = overbought, likely to pull back SELL
⏳
Trade not working after 15-30 min — if it hasn't moved in your favor, cut it. Dead money. EXIT

Chart Patterns — Quick ID

Bull Flag 🏁 Sharp up → tight pullback → break up BUY
Bear Flag 🚩 Sharp down → tight bounce → break down SHORT
Ascending Triangle 📐 Flat top + higher lows → breaks up BUY
Double Bottom (W) Two bounces off same support → break up BUY
Double Top (M) Two rejections at same resistance → break down SELL
Cup & Handle ☕ Round bottom + small pullback → break up BUY
Head & Shoulders Left peak, higher peak, right peak → neckline break SELL
Wedge (Rising) Converging higher highs/lows → usually breaks DOWN WAIT
Wedge (Falling) Converging lower highs/lows → usually breaks UP BUY

What to AVOID

❌
Trading the first 5 minutes — Pure chaos. Let the open settle. Wait for a setup.
❌
Low volume stocks — Hard to exit. Wide spreads. Unpredictable. Stay with liquid names.
❌
Trading during lunch (12-2 PM ET) — Low volume, choppy, false moves. Take a break.
❌
Holding through earnings — Binary event. Gamblers flip. Smart traders avoid or reduce position.
❌
Averaging down on a loser — "It'll come back" = famous last words. Cut and move on.
❌
Trading without a stop-loss — One bad trade without a stop can erase a month of gains.
❌
Following "hot tips" on social media — By the time you see it, you're the exit liquidity.
❌
Trading when emotional — Angry, tired, excited, desperate = BAD trades. Walk away.
