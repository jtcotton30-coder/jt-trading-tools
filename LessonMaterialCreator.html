🚀 Publish Checklist

Step-by-step guide to get your material live on Gumroad

✅ Pre-Publish Checklist

Click each step when complete.

📋 Gumroad Launch Steps
Step 1
Create your Gumroad account
Go to gumroad.com → Sign up free. No monthly fee. Gumroad takes 10% per sale.
Step 2
Export your material as PDF
Use Export → PDF in Create New. Polish in Word or Google Docs if needed.
Step 3
Create a new product on Gumroad
Click "New Product" → Upload PDF → Set price using the Pricing Guide → Write a compelling description.
Step 4
Write your product description
Lead with the problem it solves. List exactly what's included. Add your credentials. 150–300 words ideal.
Step 5
Create a thumbnail (Canva — free)
Search "ebook cover" in Canva. Use red/black to match your brand. A good cover dramatically increases sales.
Step 6
Publish and share
Hit Publish → Share on LinkedIn, r/occupationalhealth, EHS LinkedIn groups, safety forums.
Step 7
Save your Gumroad URL in the Creator
Go to Create New → open your material → paste your Gumroad link in the Sale URL field → track revenue in Sales Tracker.
